Breif Bio
I'm a Boya Postdoc of the School of Computer Science at Peking University supervised by Prof. Tiejun Huang. I received the Everest Class of Computer Science, Sichuan University, Chengdu, China, in 2017 and the Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science, Peking University, China, in 2022. My research interests include areas of neuromorphic computing, computer vision, machine learning and spiking neural networks.
